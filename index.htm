Schoolwork

With her concentration in artificial intelligence and machine learning, Jaci's schoolwork has focused on those topics, as well as robotics. In addition, she has done work with Java, Python, Linux, and web development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. In addition to her classwork, she is vice-president of her school's chapter of Society of Women Engineers, as well as treasurer of their chapter of Association for Computing Machinery.


Internship

Jaci's internship with the Department of Defense was a life changing experience. Here, she developed CHRIS-bot (Chemistry-Human Robot Interaction System robot). CHRIS-bot is capable of assisting in field tasks in the event of natural disasters. A paper on her work is currently in the review process of the Army Research Laboratory.


Rainbow Cafe LGBTQ Center

For years, Jaci has been an activist for the LGBTQ+ community, specifically its youth. It was an easy decision to join Rainbow Cafe LGBTQ Center's board, where she has served as Human Relations chair and as secretary on the executive board.
